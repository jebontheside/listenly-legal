Privacy Policy

Listenly — Last updated: April 2026

Listenly is a couples emotional check-in app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

  • Your email address and account credentials
  • Your display name
  • Mood check-ins you submit
  • Reactions and acknowledgments between you and your partner
  • Notification preferences and timezone

How we use it

  • To deliver your check-ins and reactions to your partner
  • To generate AI-powered connection suggestions. Check-in content is sent to Anthropic’s Claude API to generate these suggestions. By using the suggestion feature, you consent to this. You can decline consent during onboarding.
  • To send push notifications
  • To authenticate your account. If you sign in with Apple or Google, your authentication is processed by that provider.

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell your data
  • We don’t use your data for advertising
  • We don’t share your data with anyone except Anthropic (for AI suggestions), Apple (for push notifications and, if chosen, authentication), and Google (if chosen, for authentication)

Data storage

Your data is stored securely via Supabase (us-east-1).

Account deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app under Settings. Deletion is permanent.

Contact

Questions? Email us at listenlyapp@gmail.com.
